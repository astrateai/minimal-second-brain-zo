# Client FAQ (Noob-Friendly)

## Why do I need this?

Because your ideas and notes are currently scattered.
This gives you one memory lane: you drop stuff in, AI organizes it, and you can ask better questions later.

## What do I actually do?

One job: send source material to `raw/`.

## Where is my raw folder?

`/home/workspace/Wiki/<client-slug>-wiki/raw`

## I’m on my phone. Do I need to browse folders?

No. Easiest options:

1. Paste text in chat and say: "Save this to my raw folder."
2. Text Zo and say: "Save this note to my raw folder."
3. Upload file/image in chat and say: "Save this to raw."

## What happens after I send stuff?

Zo compiles it into your wiki, answers questions from it, and sends weekly digests to keep it active.
